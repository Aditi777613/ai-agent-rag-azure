COMPANY — PRODUCT FAQ
Product: AI Agent Platform (Enterprise Workflow Automation)
Last Updated: March 2025

Q1: What is AI Agent Platform?
A: AI Agent Platform is an enterprise workflow automation platform that helps organizations streamline their business processes. It provides a drag-and-drop interface for building custom workflows, integrates with 200+ third-party applications, and includes built-in analytics and reporting capabilities.

Q2: What are the pricing tiers?
A: The platform offers three pricing tiers:
- Starter: $29/user/month — Up to 10 users, 50 workflows, email support, basic integrations
- Professional: $79/user/month — Up to 100 users, unlimited workflows, priority support, advanced integrations, API access, custom branding
- Enterprise: Custom pricing — Unlimited users, dedicated account manager, SLA guarantees, SSO/SAML, on-premise deployment option, custom SLA

Q3: What integrations are supported?
A: The platform integrates with popular tools including Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Jira, GitHub, Google Workspace, SAP, Oracle, ServiceNow, Zendesk, and 190+ other applications. Custom integrations can be built using our REST API and webhook support.

Q4: Is the platform SOC 2 compliant?
A: Yes, the platform is SOC 2 Type II certified. We undergo annual third-party audits. We also comply with GDPR, HIPAA (with BAA), and CCPA. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We maintain 99.95% uptime SLA for Professional and Enterprise tiers.

Q5: How is data residency handled?
A: The platform offers data residency options in the United States, European Union, and Asia-Pacific regions. Enterprise customers can choose their preferred data center location during onboarding. Data does not leave the selected region unless explicitly configured by the customer's administrator.

Q6: What is the onboarding process?
A: The standard onboarding process includes:
1. Discovery call with a solutions architect (1 hour)
2. Environment setup and configuration (1-2 business days)
3. Data migration assistance (if applicable, 3-5 business days)
4. Admin training session (2 hours)
5. End-user training session (1 hour)
6. Go-live support with dedicated CSM for first 30 days
Enterprise customers receive extended onboarding with custom training programs.

Q7: What level of support is provided?
A: Support levels vary by tier:
- Starter: Email support, 48-hour response time, knowledge base access
- Professional: Email + chat support, 4-hour response time, phone support during business hours
- Enterprise: 24/7 phone, email, and chat support, 1-hour response time for critical issues, dedicated Technical Account Manager

Q8: Can the platform work offline?
A: The platform is cloud-based and requires an internet connection for full functionality. However, the Enterprise tier offers an on-premise deployment option for organizations with strict connectivity requirements. A hybrid mode is available where certain approved workflows can be cached locally and synced when connectivity is restored.

Q9: How do I request a new feature?
A: Feature requests can be submitted through the Community Portal. Requests are reviewed quarterly by our product team. Enterprise customers can request features directly through their Technical Account Manager with prioritized review.

Q10: What is the cancellation policy?
A: Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time with 30 days notice. Annual plans can be cancelled with 60 days notice before the renewal date. A pro-rated refund is available for annual plans cancelled within the first 90 days. Enterprise contracts follow the terms specified in the individual agreement.
